Membership Dues

Annual dues are $300 per parcel. All homeowners are automatically required to follow the CC&R’s and Architectural Guidelines of the CRHOA whether they pay dues or not. Dues-paying members are extremely valuable to the neighborhood as this organization could not function without them!

Credit cards and PayPal  are accepted.  To pay, please select the number of parcels you would like to pay for using the dropdown box and type the addresses for those parcels using the text box below.

Then, click Buy Now to complete your purchase on PayPal.
